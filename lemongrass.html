Description

Bumbleroot’s Baobab Drink mixes combine real, healthy foods from around the world to create an everyday hydration drink with delicious, subtle flavor that comes in a convenient compact pouch, ready to add to your water bottle, water glass, or mason jar. 

Loaded with electrolytes, courtesy of the coconut water, baobab, and sea salt, the drinks help you stay hydrated when you’re out playing or during your active day.  The antioxidants provided by the baobab help counter free radicals. Did we mention they are only 25 calories each?

And by choosing a drink made from real plants, rather than unpronounceable ingredients made in a plant, you are voting for a more sustainable food system.
